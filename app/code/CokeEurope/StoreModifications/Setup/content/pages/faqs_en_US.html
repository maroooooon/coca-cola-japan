Are all your printing and delivery methods sustainable?

We are committed to removing all unnecessary packaging for the production and distribution of your order. The personalized design is printed directly on the chosen product, eliminating an additional sleeve. We are working closely with partners to provide the most sustainable method and packaging to deliver the item to you safely and securely.

How long will it take to receive my order?

This depends on the time of day you submit your order. In most cases, your order will be created the next day and dispatched at the earliest possible time. We anticipate you receiving your order within 5-7 working days.

How much will it cost to personalise a bottle or can? Can I also customise PET?

We are currently offering personalisation on 330ml cans and 200ml glass bottle in selected markets. Each can will cost €5 plus postage and the bottle will cost €5.50 plus postage.

Are the amount of products limited that I can order

There are no limitations to the amount of bottles or cans you can order.

Can I send a personalized product to someone else? 

Yes, the site will ask for a delivery address, which you can post to someone else.

Can I send a personalized product to another country

Unfortunately no, we can only ship the product to the country the website is dedicated for.

What is the character limit?

Messaging on screen, will inform how many characters per SKU and how will be remaining.

How can I track my order? 

A tracking number is provided through a delivery confirmation email, allowing you to track your order.

Is the process easy to use? 

Yes, there are just a few simple steps – go to website, login / register, choose your brand, desired pack, preferred design, add your statement, preview and confirm and fill in payment details.

Who can I contact if I have trouble with my order? 

Contact information is provided on the site.

How do I pay? 

All major credit & debit cards are accepted (also Bancontact, iDeal). If you have a voucher, this can also be redeemed at checkout.

Why do I have to register to place an order?

There are some details that we require from you in order to fulfill your order successfully and keep you updated with shipping information.

How do I sign up for a Coca-Cola account?

You can create an account on a brand website, on Coca-Cola Store and on the Coca-Cola mobile app. You can register with your email address or through Sign in with Google, or Facebook by clicking on the head and shoulders icon in the top right corner of this website.

Where can I find out how you will be handling my data?

Please refer to the Privacy Policy here.

How are the delivery costs calculated?

There will be a set amount for single delivery and free delivery after a value has been reached.

Can I drink the product?

Absolutely – the products we sell on the site are drinkable. Please refer to the expiry date on the can or bottle.

Why was the name or phrase I entered for a personalized product denied?

Coca-Cola reserves the right to deny any names or phrases on customized cans, bottles or merchandise. Oftentimes, verbiage is declined due to double meanings or cultural and religious sensitivities. We are only able to include trademarked names or phrases if given expressed permission from the trademark holder. Unfortunately, we cannot feedback on the detail of why your bespoke request has been rejected. You are welcome to request an alternative bespoke pledge and/or name.

How long will it take to find out if my bespoke request has been accepted or rejected?

We will aim to reply to your request within 3-4 working days to inform you if your request has been accepted or rejected.

I have a voucher code, can I order a personalized product  for free?

One voucher code will entitle you to one free personalized bottle and the associated delivery costs for that one can. Voucher codes are one time use only. You can add more products to your order if you would like, but the voucher is only applied to 1 product.

How does Coca-Cola treat my Personal information?

We use standard physical, technical and administrative measures designed to reduce the risk of loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure or modification of your Personal Information. Check out our Privacy Policy if you’d like to find out more.
